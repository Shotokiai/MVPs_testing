FAQ

Questions schools always ask.

How long does it take to build the website? +
Typically 2–3 weeks from the day I receive your content. The biggest variable is how quickly you can send me photos, text, and approvals. I send you a clear content checklist on Day 1 so nothing gets missed.
Can our staff update the website after delivery? +
Depends on the plan. Standard and Premium plans use WordPress — your admin can update text and photos easily through a simple dashboard. Starter plan websites are static HTML — fast, lightweight, and purpose-built for nurseries where content rarely changes. For any updates, just WhatsApp me and it's done within 24 hours.
Will our school appear on Google after the website is live? +
I set up the technical SEO foundation — page titles, Google Search Console, sitemap, and meta descriptions. Google typically takes 3–6 months to index and rank new websites. I cannot guarantee a specific ranking position, but the foundation I build gives your school the best possible starting point.
What are the payment terms? +
50% advance before I start building. 50% on delivery, before the website goes live on your domain. Payment via UPI, bank transfer, or cheque. I send a simple 1-page project agreement before we begin so everything is clear in writing.
What if we want changes after the website is live? +

After delivery, any changes are available on a simple per-request basis — no retainer required unless you want one:

  • Small changes (update phone number, add a photo, change a date, fix a typo) — ₹0, covered under a 30-day support window post-delivery.
  • Content updates after 30 days — ₹300–500 per change, WhatsApp me and it's done within 24 hours.
  • Larger changes (add a new page, redesign a section) — quoted separately based on scope.
